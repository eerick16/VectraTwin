# Transport Modes

## Maritime

Advantages:

- Lowest cost per ton
- Massive cargo capacity

Disadvantages:

- Slow transit times
- Weather sensitivity
- Port dependency

Typical Speed:

20-25 knots

---

## Air

Advantages:

- Fastest transport mode

Disadvantages:

- Highest operational cost

Typical Speed:

800-900 km/h

---

## Rail

Advantages:

- Stable transit times
- Efficient fuel usage

Disadvantages:

- Fixed infrastructure
- Excessive pollution

Typical Speed:

60-80 km/h

---

## Road

Advantages:

- Highest flexibility

Disadvantages:

- Traffic
- Driver regulations

Typical Speed:

80-100 km/h
